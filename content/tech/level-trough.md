## Procedure

2. Remove obvious dirt, damage, or unsuitable portions.
3. Prepare only enough for a trial.
4. Use it on scrap or a low-value task.

## Verification and quality control

A useful level trough batch or tool gives similar results when repeated from the same source. Check fit, strength, wear, contamination, cracking, loosening, shrinkage, or other visible failure after first use. Compare against a retained reference sample before scaling up.
